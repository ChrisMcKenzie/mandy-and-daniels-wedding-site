Bride’s story

Amanda McKenzie tiptoed into our lives on October 17, 1994 at Foothill Presbyterian Hospital in Glendora. Always a very sweet, and bashful child. Amanda began her love of baking at a very young age and also began Riding horses the beginning of a bolder Amanda; High School proved to further her step out of her comfort zone as she was Class President three years running.

Amanda continued her education at the Art Institute where she received her Associates in Science this past December. Daniel Brewster stepped into her life three years ago and swept her off her feet Amanda has fallen madly in love with him, and looks forward to spending the rest of her life with her one true love.

“In spite of everything I still believe that people are really good at heart”

Groom’s story

Daniel Ray Brewster was born on April 24, 1992 in Fontana, CA to Paul & Linda. He has a brother, John, and a sister, Katie. Daniel was a Cub Scout and Boy Scout, Daniel has a huge love of the outdoors hiking and playing various sports as a youth. He graduated from Alta Loma High School, where he excelled in Track and Wrestling. Daniel attended Chaffey College after high school, and currently works at Turners Outdoorsman as a warehouse clerk in the gun safe. He met the love of his life, Amanda, in July of 2012 at Corona Del Mar Beach, and they have been together ever since. Daniel’s entire family is very happy for the two of them, and wishes them a wonderful life together.
